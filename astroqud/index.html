Inquisitive Questions from Scientific Figures

Scientific figure QA benchmarks usually ask shallow, perceptual questions — What's the value of the red bar? How many lines are in the legend? The examples below are different. Each pairs a figure with the kind of inquisitive question a curious reader actually asks in context — Why does this diverge? What does that shape imply? How do these really compare? — and the answer drawn from the paper's text. Click a card to expand for the full context.
