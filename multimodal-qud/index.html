Multimodal QUD:

Inquisitive Questions from Scientific Figures

Three questions you could ask about a scientific figure:

  • ChartQA Which year has the highest unemployment rate?
  • SPIQA What evaluation metric is used in Figure 2?
  • Multimodal QUD Why does the attention shift to the image after the intervention?

The first two you can answer just from the figure. The third needs the figure and the paragraphs of text around it. That's the gap this dataset is for. Click a card to expand.
