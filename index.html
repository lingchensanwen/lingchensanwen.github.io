Yating Wu

Hi, y'all, I'm a Ph.D. candidate from the ECE department at UT Austin. I'm supervised by Prof. Jessy Li and Prof. Alex Dimakis. My research interest focus are natural language processing and computational linguisitic. Outside research, I love soccer, table tennis and Japanese. You can find my CV here.

Research Overview

  • Build computational frameworks to reveal implicit reasoning structures and trace how ideas connect in text, specifically through Questions Under Discussion.
  • Develop evaluation methods to enable better and more efficient model training through structured reasoning feedback.
  • Apply structured reasoning to real-world tasks such as summarization, retrieval, and elaborative text simplification.
  • Advance LLM reasoning for scientific discovery as part of CosmicAI.
